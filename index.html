About Project Atlantis

The Mission

Our mission is to learn more about Atlantis, Atlanteans, and eventually make contact with their people and coexist.




The Beginning

Alexander and Coswell were the last offical and confirmed individuals to make contact with the atlantean race. After their encounter they decided to remap their life mission to uncovering the mystery behind the atlantean race (which after the initial has been unsuccessful) as well as their home - Atlantis. Both underwent extensive personal transformations intellectually and spiritually to further persue contact and gather new information. Much of their documentation has been lost or confiscated since the death of both but their legacy lives on. This organization was founded in spring of 1793 by Alexander and this is what remains.





The Founders

Founded in 1790 and established in 1793 by business partners and Friends, Coswell Owens the Second and Alexander A. Jhonasna. They were the last official and confirmed human beings to make contact with the atlanteans Further information can be provided in their biographies and Alexander's book: Consuquentio La Atlantean.
